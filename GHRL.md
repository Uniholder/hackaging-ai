## 🔬 2. Structure and Functional Domains
- **Protein Length:** 117 amino acids (canonical isoform)
- **Key Domains / Motifs:**
  - Signal peptide (1–23)
  - Ghrelin-28 bioactive peptide (24–51)
  - Obestatin (76–98)
  - Disordered region (29–50)
  - Helix structure (32–36, PDB: 7W2Z)
- **Functional Roles:** Regulates appetite, growth hormone release, and energy homeostasis via GHSR activation; modulates insulin secretion and metabolic pathways.
- **Post-Translational Modifications (PTMs):**
  - O-octanoylation at Ser-26 (essential for ghrelin activity, mediated by GOAT/MBOAT4)
  - Leucine amidation at Leu-98 (essential for obestatin activity)
- **Orthologs / Paralogs:** No explicit ortholog data provided in KEGG/UniProt sources.

## 💊 6. Small Molecule and Drug Interactions
- **Key Modulators:**
  - GOAT (MBOAT4) inhibitors: Block O-octanoylation of Ser-26, abolishing ghrelin activity.
  - Ghrelin receptor (GHSR) agonists/antagonists: Modulate appetite and growth hormone release.
- **Mechanisms:**
  - Acylation status determines receptor activation.
  - No specific small-molecule drugs documented in KEGG/UniProt sources.
