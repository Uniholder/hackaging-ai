- **Key Domains / Motifs:**
  - Signal peptide (1–21)
  - Propeptide (22–48)
  - Mature chain (49–118) with subregions:
    - Region B (49–77)
    - Region C (78–89)
    - Region A (90–110)
    - Region D (111–118)
  - E peptide (119–195; disordered)
  - Disulfide bonds: Cys54–Cys96, Cys66–Cys109, Cys95–Cys100

# 🧠 4. Pathways and Functional Networks
- **KEGG Pathways:**
  - [PI3K-Akt signaling](https://www.kegg.jp/pathway/hsa04151): Central to aging, autophagy, and oxidative stress resistance.
  - [Dilated cardiomyopathy](https://www.kegg.jp/pathway/hsa05414): Regulates cardiac muscle survival and hypertrophy.
  - [EGFR inhibitor resistance](https://www.kegg.jp/pathway/hsa01521): Activates parallel survival pathways in cancer.
- **Interaction Partners:**
  - IGF1R (primary receptor)
  - Integrins (αvβ3, α5β1)
  - IGFBP3 (binding protein modulating bioavailability)
- **Biological Roles:** Mediates crosstalk between growth factor signaling, metabolic regulation, and stress response pathways.

# 🧓 5. Longevity and Aging Associations
- **Lifespan Effects:**
  - **Extension:** IGF1 knockout extends lifespan in female mice (16.1%) and *Drosophila* (33.5%).
  - **Reduction:** IGF1 knockout shortens lifespan in male mice (27.5%) and *C. elegans* with extended baseline longevity.
  - **Tissue-Specific:** Cardiac-specific overexpression increases mouse lifespan by 23%.
- **Healthspan Improvements:** Enhanced stress resistance (starvation, heat) and metabolic health in flies.

| Model | Intervention | Result | Reference |
|--------|--------------|--------|------------|
| Mouse (♀) | Whole-body knockout | ↑ Lifespan (16.1%) | [OpenGenes](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1474-9726.2009.00493.x) |
| *Drosophila* | Dilp2-GAL4-driven knockdown | ↑ Median lifespan (33.5%) | [OpenGenes](https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2318-9-19) |
| Mouse (♂) | Cardiac-specific overexpression | ↑ Lifespan (23%) | [OpenGenes](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11357-011-9210-z) |
| Human (♂) | *IGF1R* rs2229765 A/A genotype | ↓ Plasma IGF-1, ↑ longevity | [PMID:19473235](https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2318-9-19) |

# 💊 6. Small Molecule and Drug Interactions
- **Masoprocol (D04862):** Linked to IGF1 in KEGG; may modulate oxidative stress pathways downstream of IGF1 signaling [[KEGG:D04862](https://www.kegg.jp/entry/D04862)].
- **Mechanism:** Potential indirect modulation of IGF1-mediated PI3K-Akt activation.
- **Note:** Direct binding evidence is limited; interactions likely occur through pathway crosstalk.

---

# 🌍 7. Evolutionary Conservation
- **Domain Conservation:** Cysteine residues (disulfide bonds) and receptor-binding regions (Region A) are highly conserved from *C. elegans* to humans.
- **Ortholog Functions:**
  - *C. elegans* SKN-1: Regulates oxidative stress response and lifespan.
  - *Drosophila* CncC: Mediates detoxification and longevity.
- **Longevity Mechanism:** Reduced IGF1 signaling extends lifespan across species, but sex-specific effects (e.g., male mice) highlight evolutionary divergence in aging regulation.
