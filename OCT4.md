# 🔬 2. Structure and Functional Domains
- **Protein Length:** 360 amino acids (canonical isoform)
- **Key Domains / Motifs:**
  - **POU-specific domain** (138–212): Mediates interaction with PKM
  - **Homeobox domain** (230–289): Binds octamer motif (5'-ATTTGCAT-3')
  - **9aaTAD motif** (4–12): Transactivation domain essential for transcriptional activity
  - **Disordered regions** (1–52, 88–114): Regulatory flexibility
- **Functional Roles:** Master regulator of pluripotency in embryonic stem cells; maintains self-renewal and suppresses differentiation.
- **Post-Translational Modifications (PTMs):**
  - Phosphorylation at S111 (nuclear exclusion), T235/S236 (reduced DNA binding)
  - Sumoylation at K123 (enhanced stability/DNA binding)
  - Ubiquitination by WWP2 (proteasomal degradation)
- **Orthologs / Paralogs:**
  - *C. elegans*: SKN-1 (35% identity in DNA-binding domains)
  - *Drosophila*: CncC (conserved POU-homeodomain structure)

# 🧠 4. Pathways and Functional Networks
- **KEGG Pathway:** [hsa04550: Signaling pathways regulating pluripotency of stem cells](https://www.kegg.jp/pathway/hsa04550)
- **Key Interactions:** Forms core regulatory network with SOX2 and NANOG to maintain stem cell pluripotency
- **Biological Roles:**
  - Orchestrates embryonic development and germ layer specification
  - Regulates metabolic reprogramming in stem cells via PKM interaction
  - Integrates MAPK signaling through phosphorylation-dependent localization

# 🧓 5. Longevity and Aging Associations
- **Indirect Longevity Links:** Associated with two hallmarks of aging: *transcriptional alterations* and *stem cell exhaustion*
- **Cellular Reprogramming Evidence:**
  - Transient OCT4 expression reverses age-related epigenetic markers in human fibroblasts
  - Partial reprogramming extends healthspan in progeroid mice models

| Model | Intervention | Result | Reference |
|--------|--------------|--------|------------|
| Human fibroblasts | Transient OCT4 induction | ↓ DNA methylation age, ↑ mitochondrial function | [OpenGenes](https://opengenes.org/gene/OCT4) |
| Progeroid mice | Cyclic OSKM expression | ↓ Senescence markers, ↑ tissue function | PMID: 27087446 |

# 💊 6. Small Molecule and Drug Interactions
- **WWP2-mediated ubiquitination:** Targeted by small-molecule inhibitors (e.g., indomethacin derivatives) to stabilize OCT4 in stem cell cultures
- **MAPK pathway modulation:** MEK inhibitors (e.g., PD0325901) reduce S111 phosphorylation, promoting nuclear retention
- **Reprogramming enhancers:** Ascorbic acid increases OCT4 stability during iPSC generation

---

# 🌍 7. Evolutionary Conservation
- **POU-homeodomain:** >90% conservation across vertebrates in DNA-contact residues (positions 157, 164)
- **Functional conservation:** *C. elegans* SKN-1 and *Drosophila* CncC maintain oxidative stress resistance roles analogous to mammalian OCT4
- **Longevity link:** SKN-1 activation extends *C. elegans* lifespan through conserved stress response pathways
