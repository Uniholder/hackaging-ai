# 🔬 2. Structure and Functional Domains
- **Protein Length:** 222 amino acids (canonical isoform)
- **Key Domains / Motifs:**
  - Mitochondrial transit peptide (1–24)
  - Mn²⁺-binding sites (50, 98, 183, 187)
  - Structural helices (44–52, 54–74, 78–83, 85–103, etc.)
  - Beta strands (144–153, 158–165, 177–183)
- **Functional Roles:** Catalyzes dismutation of superoxide radicals into oxygen and hydrogen peroxide; critical for mitochondrial oxidative stress defense (GO:0004784, GO:0034599).
- **Post-Translational Modifications (PTMs):**
  - Y58: 3'-nitrotyrosine (inhibits activity under oxidative stress)
  - K122: N6-acetyllysine (decreases activity; deacetylated by SIRT3 during fasting/radiation)
  - Polyubiquitination (regulated by USP36 for proteasomal degradation)
- **Orthologs / Paralogs:**
  - *C. elegans*: sod-2 (54% lifespan increase with knockout)
  - *Drosophila*: MnSOD (34.5% lifespan increase with overexpression)

# 🧠 4. Pathways and Functional Networks
- **KEGG Pathways:**
  - [Lipid and Atherosclerosis (hsa05417)](https://www.kegg.jp/pathway/hsa05417): SOD2 mitigates vascular oxidative stress, preventing LDL oxidation and endothelial dysfunction.
  - [FoxO Signaling Pathway (hsa04068)](https://www.kegg.jp/pathway/hsa04068): Transcriptionally regulated by FoxO factors; enhances antioxidant defenses linked to longevity.
- **Interaction Partners:**
  - KEAP1 (indirect via oxidative stress response)
  - SIRT3 (deacetylates K122)
  - USP36 (deubiquitinates SOD2)
- **Biological Roles:** Central to mitochondrial ROS detoxification, metabolic stress response, and aging-related pathways.

# 💊 6. Small Molecule and Drug Interactions
- **SIRT3 Activators:** Deacetylate K122, restoring SOD2 activity during metabolic stress.
- **EUK-134/EUK-008:** Catalytic antioxidants mimicking SOD2 function; extend lifespan in *C. elegans*.
- **USP36 Modulation:** Stabilizes SOD2 by preventing proteasomal degradation.
- **Mechanism Example:** SIRT3 activation → K122 deacetylation → enhanced SOD2 activity → reduced mitochondrial ROS → improved stress resistance.

---

# 🌍 7. Evolutionary Conservation
- **Conserved Motifs:** Mn²⁺-binding sites (100% conserved from bacteria to humans), mitochondrial transit peptides.
- **Orthologs:**
  - *C. elegans*: sod-2 (lifespan extension with knockout)
  - *Drosophila*: MnSOD (lifespan extension with overexpression)
  - *Mus musculus*: Sod2 (essential for viability; knockout causes neonatal lethality)
- **Functional Conservation:** ROS detoxification role preserved across eukaryotes, but longevity effects vary by organism (pro-longevity in invertebrates, essential for survival in mammals).
