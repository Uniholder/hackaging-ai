## 🔬 2. Structure and Functional Domains
- **Protein Length:** 317 amino acids
- **Key Domains / Motifs:**
  - **HMG box** (41–109): DNA-binding domain forming trimeric complexes with OCT4 on enhancer regions (e.g., *NANOG*, *FGF4*)
  - **9aaTAD motif** (272–280): Transactivation domain critical for transcriptional activity
  - **Disordered regions** (1–43, 243–266, 297–317): Intrinsically disordered regions facilitating protein interactions
  - **Low complexity region** (246–261): Compositional bias with regulatory roles
- **Functional Roles:** Master regulator of pluripotency in stem cells; essential for embryonic development, neural differentiation, and maintenance of self-renewal
- **Post-Translational Modifications (PTMs):**
  - **K42me/K117me**: N6-methyllysine regulating proteasomal degradation and protein stability
  - **S251phos**: Phosphorylation during stem cell differentiation
  - **K245 sumoylation**: Inhibits DNA binding and *FGF4* transactivation
- **Orthologs / Paralogs:** HMG box domain is highly conserved across SOX family proteins; no specific orthologs listed in provided data

## 🧠 4. Pathways and Functional Networks
- **KEGG Pathway:** [hsa04550: Signaling pathways regulating pluripotency of stem cells](https://www.kegg.jp/pathway/hsa04550)
  - SOX2 forms core regulatory circuitry with **OCT4** and **NANOG** to maintain embryonic stem cell pluripotency
  - Critical for suppressing differentiation and sustaining self-renewal in regenerative contexts
  - Interacts with WNT, TGF-β, and FGF signaling pathways during development
- **Disease Link:** Mutations cause [Microphthalmia, syndromic (H02170)](https://www.kegg.jp/entry/H02170) due to disrupted eye development

---

## 🧓 5. Longevity and Aging Associations
- **Aging Relevance:** SOX2 protects against age-related impairment through stem cell maintenance and regulation of cellular reprogramming
- **Key Mechanisms:**
  - Counteracts **stem cell exhaustion**, a hallmark of aging
  - Modulates transcriptional networks involved in tissue regeneration
- **Limitations:** No direct lifespan extension/reduction data in model organisms or human population studies per OpenGenes database

---

## 🌍 7. Evolutionary Conservation
- **HMG box domain** is evolutionarily conserved across metazoans, with structural homology in *Drosophila* Dichaete and *C. elegans* SOX-2
- **Functional conservation:** DNA-binding mechanism preserved from invertebrates to mammals, though longevity-specific roles are less characterized outside mammals
